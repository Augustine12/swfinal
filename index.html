Kindness

“My wish for you is that you continue. Continue to be who and how you are, to astonish a mean world with your acts of kindness. Continue to allow humor to lighten the burden of your tender heart.”
― Maya Angelou



Compassion

“You may not control all the events that happen to you, but you can decide not to be reduced by them. Try to be a rainbow in someone's cloud. Do not complain. Make every effort to change things you do not like. If you cannot make a change, change the way you have been thinking. You might find a new solution.”
― Maya Angelou



Graditude

“Each of us has the right and the responsibility to assess the roads which lie ahead, and those over which we have traveled, and if the future road looms ominous or unpromising, and the roads back uninviting, then we need to gather our resolve and, carrying only the necessary baggage, step off that road into another direction. If the new choice is also unpalatable, without embarrassment, we must be ready to change that as well.”
― Maya Angelou

Manifesto

Eliòe Augustine

As an artist, I have always been intrigued by virtues; in particular, how they intertwine
within human society.

Kindness
Compassion
Graditude

I go about life collecting lists of kindness and compassion, so... I can make a bigger list for Gratitude.

Gradtitude propels me to give and live freely. Giving and living freeely allows me to own
who I am and what I create!
